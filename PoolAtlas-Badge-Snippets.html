Pool Atlas — Badge Email Snippets

One-click copy for embedding each hotel's ranked badge in your outreach email.

How to embed a badge in your email (Zoho): Click "Copy HTML" next to any hotel. In Zoho Compose, switch to HTML/Source mode (the </> icon in the toolbar), paste the snippet where you want the badge to appear, then switch back to Normal view. The badge will display as a clickable image that links to the hotel's Pool Atlas page.
