How to add this to Zoho Mail

  1. Open Zoho Mail and go to Settings (gear icon, top right)
  2. Click Signatures in the left sidebar
  3. Click Add New Signature
  4. Switch to HTML mode (look for the </> or "Source" toggle)
  5. Click the "Copy HTML" button below, then paste into the editor
  6. Save and set as your default signature
