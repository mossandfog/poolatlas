# Pool Atlas — Hotel PR Outreach Kit

## How to use this kit

1. Find the hotel in the table below and note their **rank**, **pool page URL**, and **badge URL**
2. Find the PR or marketing contact for the hotel (LinkedIn, hotel website "Press" page, or media@[hotelname].com)
3. Personalize the email template with the hotel's name, rank, and property details
4. Send from hello@poolatlas.io or your personal address with Pool Atlas context in the signature

## Tips for outreach

- **Who to contact:** "PR", "Marketing", "Communications", or "Digital Marketing" at the property. For smaller independent hotels, the General Manager often handles this directly. LinkedIn is usually the fastest way to find a name.
- **Subject line matters:** Use the rank number — "[Hotel] ranked #3 in the world" is an irresistible subject line for a PR person.
- **Follow-up:** If you don't hear back in 10 days, one follow-up is appropriate. Keep it short: "Just bumping this up in case it got buried — happy to answer any questions."
- **High-value targets:** Top 10 hotels are most likely to engage because the recognition is most significant. Focus here first.
- **Social amplification:** After emailing, tag the hotel on Instagram and X/Twitter with a photo and "We've ranked [Hotel] #[X] in the world for hotel pools." Hotel social teams often reshare this even without the email relationship.

## Suggested outreach order

Start with the hotels most likely to engage and amplify:

1. **Independent / boutique hotels** (Monastero Santa Rosa, Nayara, Rock House, Villa d'Este, Hermitage Bay) — smaller teams, GM often personally handles PR, faster response time
2. **Belmond properties** — they have a central PR team that manages all properties collectively; one email can cover Belmond Hotel Caruso and Copacabana Palace
3. **Four Seasons** — large brand with property-level PR contacts; high social media presence
4. **Aman properties** — famously private brand, but their PR team does engage editorial rankings
5. **Address/Atlantis/Dubai properties** — very active in PR and social, likely to reshare
