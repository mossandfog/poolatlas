# Pool Atlas Hotel Outreach Email

## Subject Line Options (A/B test these):
- "Your pool made our list"
- "[Hotel Name] - You're on our radar"
- "An invitation from Pool Atlas"
- "We're ranking the world's best pools. You're on the list."

---

## Email Body

**Subject:** Your pool made our list

Hi [First Name],

I'm reaching out because [Hotel Name]'s pool is extraordinary — and we've taken notice.

I'm the founder of **Pool Atlas**, a new editorial platform dedicated to one thing: finding and ranking the world's most exceptional hotel pools. Think of us as the Michelin Guide, but for pools.

We've spent the past year researching pools across 50+ countries, compiling data from publications like Condé Nast Traveler, Travel + Leisure, and Forbes, combined with our own proprietary criteria around design, setting, amenities, and guest experience.

**[Hotel Name] is being considered for our rankings.**

We're currently finalizing our Top 100 list and reaching out to a small group of properties we believe represent the best in the world. This isn't a pay-to-play directory — our rankings are editorial and independent.

That said, we're also selectively partnering with a handful of properties who want to do more:

- **Featured Profile**: An in-depth story on your pool, your design philosophy, and what makes it special
- **"Pool of the Month" Spotlight**: Premium homepage placement with stunning photography
- **Badge Program**: A "Pool Atlas Ranked" badge for your marketing materials

If you'd like to learn more — or simply confirm details about your property for our database — I'd love to connect.

You can explore the site here: **poolatlas.io**

Either way, congratulations on building something worth celebrating.

Best,
[Your Name]
Founder, Pool Atlas
hello@poolatlas.io

P.S. We're launching our first annual "World's Best Pools" report this spring. Properties confirmed by [date] will be included in the inaugural edition.

## Tips for Maximum Response Rate

1. **Personalize the first line** - Mention something specific about their pool (the infinity edge, the view, the design architect)

2. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am** in the recipient's timezone

3. **Keep it under 200 words** for the initial outreach

4. **Use their first name** - Find it on LinkedIn if not on the website

5. **Don't attach anything** - Links only, no PDFs or images in first email

6. **Follow up exactly twice** - Day 5 and Day 12, then move on

7. **Track everything** - Use a simple spreadsheet: Name, Email, Date Sent, Response, Status

---

## What Makes This Email Work

- **Flattery without being sycophantic** - "Your pool is extraordinary" is direct and credible
- **Exclusivity** - "small group of properties" creates scarcity
- **No ask in the first paragraph** - Build intrigue first
- **Clear value proposition** - They get featured, not sold to
- **Soft sponsorship mention** - Opens the door without pressure
- **Deadline/urgency** - The P.S. creates a reason to respond now
- **Easy out** - "Either way, congratulations" removes pressure
