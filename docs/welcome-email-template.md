## Tips for Email Success

1. **Keep it personal** - Write like you're emailing a friend who loves travel
2. **Mobile-first** - Most readers open on phones, keep paragraphs short
3. **One clear CTA** - Don't overwhelm with too many links
4. **Show personality** - Pool Atlas has a voice: knowledgeable but approachable
5. **Test subject lines** - A/B test when your list grows
