# 100 Hotels with Amazing Pools - Outreach List

## How to Find Hotel Contacts

Most luxury hotels use predictable email formats. Try these patterns:
- `press@hotelname.com`
- `pr@hotelname.com`
- `media@hotelname.com`
- `marketing@hotelname.com`
- `info@hotelname.com`
- `reservations@hotelname.com` (ask to be forwarded to PR/Marketing)

**Pro Tips:**
- LinkedIn is your best friend - search for "[Hotel Name] PR Manager" or "Marketing Director"
- Check the hotel's press/media page (usually in the footer)
- For hotel groups, the corporate PR team handles multiple properties
- Hunter.io and Apollo.io can help verify email formats

## Finding Email Contacts - Tools & Techniques

1. **Hunter.io** - Enter hotel domain to find email patterns
2. **Apollo.io** - Search by company name and job title
3. **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** - Find PR/Marketing directors
4. **Hotel Press Pages** - Usually in website footer under "Media" or "Press"
5. **Press Releases** - Google "[Hotel Name] press release" to find PR contact names
6. **Instagram DMs** - Many boutique hotels respond to professional DMs
7. **Travel PR Agencies** - Many hotels use agencies like MMGY, Finn Partners, or Black Diamond
