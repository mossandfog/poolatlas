## Tips for Outreach

1. **Personalize every email** - Reference specific pool features, awards, or unique aspects
2. **Lead with value** - Mention their ranking placement and the traffic/exposure you provide
3. **Be specific about next steps** - Propose a call or specific collaboration
4. **Follow up** - Wait 5-7 business days, then send a brief follow-up
5. **Connect on LinkedIn** - Find PR/Marketing managers and connect before emailing
6. **Timing matters** - Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons
